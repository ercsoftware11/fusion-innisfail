FAQ's

You are required to book in order to attend classes. You can do this through our members portal, under the 'Schedule' tab. When you attend a class, you will also be required to 'check-in' using the 4 digit code assigned when you sign up.

Please note you will only be able to book classes once you have an active membership.

Access the members portal using the 'login' button up the top.
In order to cover our insurance and indemnity expenses, we require all members to pay $85 which will cover you for a period of 1 year.

We do however only begin this payment after 1 month of you selecting a membership, so you have a chance to try classes and get a feel for if you would like to continue.

This charge is processed manually by the staff, and will appear as a membership option on your accound automatically.
Once you have signed up and selected a membership, we will be notified and are required to approve your account. This is simply to help us prevent spam / fake accounts from signing up.

We will email you when your account is activated, so you can book your first class!
